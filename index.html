About NAGIMASEN

NAGIMASEN is a student-run food business from Mataas Na Paaralang Neptali A. Gonzales (MPNAG) that brings together the best of Filipino and Japanese cuisine. Our name embodies our mission: delivering "one bite, a thousand flavors."

We believe in offering innovative, nutritious, and culturally-authentic food experiences. Our Pinakbet Sushi Roll celebrates Filipino heritage while embracing Japanese culinary techniques, creating a unique fusion that appeals to health-conscious students and food enthusiasts alike.

All our ingredients are sourced fresh from local markets in Mandaluyong City, supporting our community while ensuring the highest quality in every bite.
